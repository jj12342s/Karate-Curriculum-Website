Cross 101

Cross Procedure
  1. Starting Stance: Maintain a balanced boxing stance with your hands up to protect your chin, elbows tucked.
  2. Footwork & Rotation: Push off your back foot, pivoting on the ball of that foot (heel lifts).
  3. Hip & Shoulder Turn: Rotate your hips and shoulders powerfully towards the target, transferring weight to your front foot.
  4. Arm Extension: Extend your rear arm straight out, aiming for the opponent's chin or nose.
  5. Fist Rotation: Twist your fist so your palm faces down (or thumb points down) just before impact, leading with your two front knuckles.
  6. Protection: Keep your lead hand up to guard your face and bring your punching shoulder up to protect your chin.
  7. Recoil: Snap your hand back to your starting guard position as quickly as you threw it.
